# Privacy Policy for Benosso Telegram Notifier

**Last updated:** May 26, 2026

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Benosso Telegram Notifier ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and protects information when you install and use our application on your Wix site.

By installing and using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

When you install and use Benosso Telegram Notifier, we collect and process the following information:

### 2.1 Site Information
- Wix Site ID
- Site owner contact email (for support purposes)

### 2.2 Order Information
When an order is placed on your Wix site, we process:
- Order ID and order number
- Order items (product names, quantities, prices)
- Order total amount and currency
- Customer name and order delivery/pickup details
- Order timestamp

### 2.3 Telegram Configuration
- Telegram Chat ID provided by you during setup
- Telegram Bot Token (stored securely)

We do **not** collect or store payment information, credit card details, or sensitive personal data beyond what is necessary to deliver order notifications.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

- To send real-time order notifications to your configured Telegram chat
- To provide, maintain, and improve the App's functionality
- To respond to your support requests
- To comply with legal obligations

## 4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We share order information with **Telegram Bot API** (operated by Telegram Messenger Inc.) for the sole purpose of delivering notifications to your chosen Telegram chat. Telegram's privacy practices are governed by their own privacy policy, available at https://telegram.org/privacy.

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your data with any other third parties for marketing or any other purpose.

## 5. Data Storage and Security

- Order data is processed in real-time and is not permanently stored on our servers
- Configuration data (Telegram Chat ID, Site ID) is stored securely using Wix's encrypted data infrastructure
- We implement industry-standard security measures to protect against unauthorized access

## 6. Data Retention

- Configuration data is retained as long as the App is installed on your site
- When you uninstall the App, all your configuration data is deleted within 30 days
- Order notification data is not persistently stored

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access the data we hold about you
- Request correction or deletion of your data
- Uninstall the App at any time, which will remove your data from our systems
- Contact us with any privacy-related concerns

## 8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
