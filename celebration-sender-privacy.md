# Privacy Policy for Benosso Celebration Sender

**Last updated:** June 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Benosso Celebration Sender ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and protects information when a store owner ("the Merchant") installs and uses the App on their site. Provider: Benosso (Osman Ali Kacar), benosso.com, osmanalikacar20@gmail.com.

By installing and using the App, the Merchant agrees to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 1. Information We Access
To function, the App accesses the following from the Merchant's own site:
- **Contact details** — first name, email address, birthday/important dates, and subscription (opt-in) status. Used to decide who to email and to personalize the message.
- **Order data** — the first order date per contact (only for the Pro "order anniversary" feature). Order contents and payment data are not read.
- **Store profile** — store name, site URL, and the logo URL the Merchant provides, used to brand the emails.
- **App settings** — the Merchant's configuration (events, templates, coupons), stored in the site's own data collections.

The App does not access payment information, passwords, or any data unrelated to sending celebration emails.

## 2. How We Use Information
- To send celebration/coupon emails to contacts who are **subscribed**.
- To create discount coupons in the Merchant's own store account (when enabled).
- To record which emails were sent (to prevent duplicates and power the monthly counter).

Emails are sent **only** to contacts whose subscription status is "Subscribed". Every email includes a one-click unsubscribe link, honored immediately and permanently.

## 3. Third Parties (Sub-processors)
We use **Resend (resend.com)** to deliver email. The recipient's email address, name, and message content are transmitted to Resend solely to deliver the message. Resend acts as a data processor. We do not sell, rent, or share data with any other third party. The App has no external dashboard or separate account.

## 4. Data Storage & Retention
- Settings and send-logs are stored in the Merchant's own site data collections on the hosting platform's encrypted infrastructure.
- Send-logs (contact id, event type, date, status) are kept to prevent duplicate sends and are removed when the App is uninstalled or upon request.
- Email delivery records are retained by Resend per Resend's own policy.

## 5. Security
- Email API credentials are stored as encrypted secrets and are never exposed to the browser or end users.
- All data transfer occurs over HTTPS/TLS.
- The sending domain is authenticated with SPF, DKIM, and DMARC.

## 6. Your Rights
Customers may unsubscribe at any time from any email. Merchants and their customers may request access to, or deletion of, their data by contacting osmanalikacar20@gmail.com. Requests are actioned within 30 days. Uninstalling the App removes its settings and logs from the site.

## 7. Children
The App is a business tool for store owners and is not directed at children.

## 8. Changes
We may update this policy; the "Last updated" date reflects any change.
